emma duhe

I am a multidisciplinary artist based in New Orleans, creating paintings and digital illustrations inspired by people, nature, and the energy of my surroundings. I’m especially drawn to bold color and capturing moments that feel personal and alive. I enjoy creating custom commissions that reflect meaningful stories and connections. My process is rooted in research, experimentation, and iteration, often exploring multiple directions and refining through feedback. Influenced by culture, community, and everyday experiences, I aim to create work that feels vibrant and engaging, encouraging viewers to feel something, stay curious, and want to see more.
