I was assigned with the task to find archived type and modernize it. My archived font came from the branding and menu of an Italian Creole restaurant in New Orleans. The restaurant was owned by my family starting in the 1920s, bought by Chef Emeril Lagasse in the late 90s, then he closed it down in the 2010s. After finding their menus from the 1920s, I was inspired to create a poster / advertisement for the restaurant as if it was going to reopen.

There is an event calendar that users will see that shows events on campus and volunteer work they can participate in, whether it is building houses or simply making monetary donations to groups in need.
